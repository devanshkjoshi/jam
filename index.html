My dearest Jam,

I still remember that evening — an ordinary pizza restaurant, a nervously beating heart, and you. I hadn't rehearsed anything. I had no grand plan. I proposed with nothing but everything I felt in that moment. And you, being perfectly, beautifully you — made me wait. Made me earn it.

When you finally said yes on the 21st of May, my whole world rearranged itself. Quietly. Completely. Into something that finally made sense.

Even through the storms — through the months we spent apart — something in me refused to let go. Because you were never just a chapter, Baccha. You are the whole story. And when we found our way back to each other in January, I understood that some things aren't lost. They're only waiting.

You are my calm in every chaos. You are the laugh I look for in every room. You are the one I'd choose again and again, in every version of this life. No matter what.

Forever yours 🌹
